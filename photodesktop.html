Photo Carousel

This example shows a photo carousel, with more than one item per page and slide transition.

Also notice the items are not just simple images, if you pass the mouse over you'll get a caption.

Scrolling is manual and it doesn't loop, if you reach the end, the "next" arrow will disappear.
